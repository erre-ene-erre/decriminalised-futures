Title: About

----

Info: <p><strong>Decriminalised Futures</strong> is a collaborative project using creative tools to explore a broad range of topics impacting on the lives of sex workers.</p><p>Our emphasis is on supporting artwork and popular education that speaks to a multiplicity of sex worker experiences; intersects with sex worker rights; and connects with broader social, labour and movement struggles.</p><p>The project is lead by organisers and artists from SWARM (Sex Worker Advocacy and Resistance Movement), in partnership with Arika and supported by the ICA.</p>
